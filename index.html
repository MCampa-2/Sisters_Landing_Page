About Me

My name is Dakota Schaffer and I am a married mother of three kids. While my husband works I am a stay at home mom who loves doing arts and crafts with my children. We live near the beach where we collect sea shells or whatever comes across our paths to use for our art. Over the years I became very skilled in making whatever I can find in nature to create beautiful art peices so I decided to make it in to a hobby of mine where I sell my art on the side. I hope you enjoy my work!
