About Me

My name is Dakota, and I'm a married mom of three wonderful kids. I'm passionate about art and I love to create unique pieces using nature as my inspiration. I spend a lot of time exploring the outdoors, collecting leaves, flowers, and rocks to use in my artwork. In fact, I love rocks so much that I often use them as the base of my creations! I find so much joy in taking natural elements and turning them into something beautiful that I can share with others.
